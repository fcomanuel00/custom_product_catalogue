How it works

1

Open the wizard

Click Catalogues → New Catalogue to launch the generator.

2

Pick products & style

Choose a filter, a pricelist, the design and which fields to include.

3

Generate & send

Download the PDF, email it to a customer, or keep it in the history for later.

A clean, focused wizard

All the options you need in one place: filters, pricelist, design, image size, the fields to show, and the optional header/footer and cover PDFs.

Style 1 — Compact table

One row per product, small thumbnail, reference, description and price. Ideal for large catalogues where space matters.

Style 2 — Full product card

Large image, prominent price, and room for a rich description. Variants appear in a tidy mini-table within the card.

Style 3 — Grid grouped by category

Three-column grid with category titles automatically inserted. Perfect for browsing a wide product range by section.

Style 4 — Configurable grid (2, 3 or 4 columns)

Pick the density you want. Tight 4-columns for a dense catalogue, spacious 2-columns for a premium look.

Full history of generated catalogues

Every catalogue is stored with its filters, style, pricelist and PDF — so you can re-download or re-send it any time without regenerating.

Built for products with variants

Each parent product is shown as a section, with its variants listed right below. Every variant carries its own attributes (color, size, etc.), its own image (with automatic fallback to the parent if not set), and its price from the selected pricelist.
